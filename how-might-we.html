How Might We?

Exploring AI applications for digital engagement

üí≠ Assignment Overview

In this collaborative exercise, you'll explore practical ways AI can enhance your digital engagement work. This is a time to think creatively, share ideas, and learn from each other's perspectives.

üéØ Your Task

  1. Gather Your Team: Discuss with your table group
  2. Explore the Questions: Consider how AI might support various aspects of your work
  3. Share Ideas: Each person should contribute their thoughts and experiences
  4. Document Insights: Take notes on the most promising ideas
  5. Report Back: Be prepared to share 1-2 key insights with the larger group

ü§î Discussion Questions

How might we use AI for...

  • Evaluating Responders: Assessing conversation quality and engagement effectiveness
  • Training Responders: Creating training materials and simulations
  • Practicing Conversations: Helping responders rehearse and improve their digital engagement skills
  • Translation: Breaking down language barriers in real-time conversations
  • Creating Ads: Developing compelling visual and written content
  • Content Localization: Adapting messages for different cultural contexts
  • Response Analysis: Understanding patterns in seeker questions and concerns
  • Follow-up Support: Personalizing ongoing engagement and discipleship
  • Resource Curation: Finding and organizing relevant content for seekers
  • Prayer Support: Organizing and responding to prayer requests
